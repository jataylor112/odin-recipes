Iceland

Rúgbrauð

A popular bread in Iceland that's often paired with other foods. It's a bread made of rye and plenty of sweetness!

Ingredients

  • Rye Flour: 3 cups
  • Whole Wheat OR All-Purpose Flour: 1 1/2 cups
  • Baking Soda: 2 1/2 teaspoons
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Kefir: 750 mL
  • Golden Syrup: 1 cup

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients (rye flour, whole wheat or all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt).
  2. Add kefir and golden syrup; you can coat the inside of the measuring jug for easy pouring of the syrup.
  3. Mix the ingredient and transfer to a form of your choice. Don’t let the dough wait too long since it might start rising.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F) and bake for 30 minutes. Lower the temperature to 100°C (212°F) and bake it for 7 hours.
