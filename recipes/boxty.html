Boxty

A traditional Irish potato pancake that descends from the north midlands of Ireland. Although it's associated with the Irish holiday of St. Brigid's Day with other traditional foods, it can be eaten on any day of the year!

Ingredients

  • Raw Potatoes, grated: 1 1/2 cups
  • All-Purpose Flour: 1 cup
  • Mashed Potatoes: 1 cup
  • Egg: 1
  • Skim Milk: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Olive Oil: 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. Toss the grated potatoes with flour in a large bowl. Stir in mashed potatoes until combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg and skim milk; mix into the potatoes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Drop in the potato mixture, forming patties about 2 inches in diameter. Fry on both sides until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate. Serve warm.
