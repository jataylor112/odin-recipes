Shopska Salata

Representing the three colors of the Bulgarian salad, the Bulgarian salad includes tomatoes, cucumbers, and white cheese with additional ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes: 4
  • Cucumbers: 2
  • Roasted Peppers or Fresh Green Bell Peppers: 2
  • Chopped Parlsey: 2 spoonfuls

    • Optional

  • Red Onion OR Green Onion: 2 spoonfuls
  • (Feta) White Cheese: 1/2 Ib. (225 g)
  • Olives
  • Salt, to taste
  • Vinegar, to taste
  • Oil, to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes, cucumbers and peppers in small pieces, cubes if possible.
  2. Mix well with the parsley, onion, salt, and oil.
  3. Serve with white cheese grated on top.
