Halloumi

Cripsy on the outside, soft on the inside, the Cypriot halloumi cheese is an excellent cheese to grill and enjoy! Enjoy with other sides or meals as desired!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Halloumi Cheese, sliced into 1/2-in pieces: 8 oz.

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Brush olive oil over both sides of each cheese slice.
  3. Place cheese directly on the grate and grill for 3 minutes. Flip and grill 3 minutes more. Serve immediately.
