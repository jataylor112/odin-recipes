Slovakia

Bryndzové Halušky

A dumpling dish common in Slovakia and considered a national dish. It contains potatoes, flour, sheep cheese, and pork fat; a hearty combination.

Ingredients

  • Raw Potatoes, peeled and finely grated: 750 g
  • Flour (hladkej múky): 250 g
  • Sheep Cheese: 250 g
  • Pork Fat or Bacon: 100 g

Steps

  1. Mix the potatoes with the flour and salt. Next, mold them into small cylindrical chunks of roughly 3 centimeters.
  2. Place them in boiling water. Once they rise to the top, check to see if one of them is thoroughly cooked. If so, remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on a warm plate.
  3. Immediately afterwards, place small chunks of sheep's milk cheese (bryndza) on top, causing it to melt over the dumplings.
  4. If preferred, sprinkle crispy pork bits on top or serve without it.

Source: Bryndzové Halušky (Slovak Dumplings with Cheese)
