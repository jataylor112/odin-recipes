Horiatiki Salad

One of the many Greek salads from the ancient islands! It contains tomatoes, onions, feta cheese, and extra-virgin olive oil. Enjoy it with a slice of crusty bread!

Ingredients

  • Large Tomatoes: 5
  • Small Red Onion, thinly sliced: 1
  • Sea Salt, to taste
  • Dried Oregano: 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • Feta Cheese, slice: 1 (8 oz.)
  • Greek Black Olives: 1/4 cup
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • (Optional) Cucumber, sliced: 1/2
  • Small Green Bell Pepper, sliced: 1

Steps

  1. Cut your tomatoes into wedges over the bowl that you're serving the salad in, so that any extra juices land in the bowl. Add onion slices. Liberally season with salt and oregano.
  2. Mix and allow tomatoes to sit at room temperature for 20 to 30 minutes, up to 2 hours, so that the salt releases the tomatoes' juices.
  3. Mix the cucumber, bell pepper, and olives just before serving. Top with feta cheese and drizzle with olive oil.
