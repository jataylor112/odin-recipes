Lutefisk with Pea Stew

An oven-baked fish fillet dish served with peas and bacon. You can also try it with brown goat cheese, syrup, or mustard!

Ingredients

  • Lutefisk: 2 kg
  • Bacon: 200 g
  • Salt: 2 tablespoons

    • Pea Stew

  • Peas, dried: 3 dl
  • Butter: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt, to taste
  • Sugar, to taste

    • Serve With:

  • Almond Potatoes: 600 g

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Cut the lutefisk into servings and place in a baking pan with skin side down.
  3. Sprinkle with salt and cover with aluminium foil or a lid.
  4. Bake the lutefisk in the oven for approx. 30 minutes. Smaller amounts of lutefisk require shorter time in the oven.
  5. Prepare the pea stew. have the peas in water over night beforehand.
  6. Cook them in fresh water until tender and the stew evens, approx. 45 minutes.
  7. Add more water if it gets too firm.
  8. Gently add butter and taste with salt and a little sugar.
  9. Serve the lutefisk with bacon, pea stew and almond potatoes.

If you want a firmer lutefisk, you can sprinkle it with salt and leave it for a couple of hours in a cool place. Drain off the water excess from the lutefisk and then rinse it in cold water.
