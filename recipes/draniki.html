Belarus

Draniki

A potato and onion pancake dish fried in pork fat that is the national dish of Belarus.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes: 2 Ibs.
  • White Onion: 1/2
  • Pork Fat: 2-4 tablespoons
  • Egg: 1
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and the onion.
  2. Finely grate one potato and the onion into a bowl and mix well. Grate the remaining potato adding them to the mixture. It should be a pulp-like consistency.
  3. Mix well, beat the egg, season it with salt and add it to the mixture.
  4. You can add a couple teaspoons of water if you would like your pancakes to be a bit crispy which I would highly recommend.
  5. Heat up the pork fat on high in a skillet and use about 1 tablespoon of mixture per fritter.
  6. Fry until golden brown on each side.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy with sour cream.
