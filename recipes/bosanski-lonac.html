Bosnia and Herzegovina

Bosanski Lonac

A Balkan stew eaten in Bosnia and Herzegovina that combines beef and potatoes with various vegetables and seasonings. Consider enjoying this meal with bread and sour cream!

Ingredients

  • Oil: 2-3 teaspoons
  • Yellow Onion, diced: 1
  • Whole Garlic Cloves peeled and pressed: 2-3
  • Beef Stew Chunks, diced: 2 Ibs.
  • Large Carrots, diced: 3
  • Green Beans, cut into 1-in pieces: 1/2 Ib.
  • Large Yellow Potatoes, diced: 3:
  • Large Tomatoes, skinned and diced: 3 (or an 8 oz. can)
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Pepper: 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Put the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. When itâ€™s hot, add the onion, garlic, and beef stirring occasionally for about 15 minutes. You can add a little water here and there to keep it simmering.
  2. Lower the temperature to medium, add carrots and continue simmering. Every few minutes add another ingredient: green beans, then potatoes, then tomatoes and sugar, all while adding a little bit of water in between.
  3. By this time, the meat has been simmering for about 30 minutes, and vegetables a little less. Add 3-4 cups of water (so it covers the ingredients, plus a little more). Add salt and pepper. Mix. Cover with lid, leaving a little bit uncovered. Lower the temperature to low, and leave to cook for 90 minutes.
