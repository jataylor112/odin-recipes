Oven Raclette

Raclette is a type of Swiss cheese that's melted for dipping vegetables, bread, meats, and other food! It's typically made on a raclette gril, which is essentially a special table-top griller to melt raclette cheese. However, this recipe utilizes an oven to make it more accessible and affordable!

Ingredients

  • Raclette Cheese: 1 1/2 Ibs
  • Thyme, chopped: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Pepper, to taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Arrange raclette cheese in six 3- to 4-inch or one 8- to 10-inch cast iron skillet(s), cutting wedges to fit as necessary.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is softened but still slightly firm in center, 5 to 10 minutes. Use a ventilation fan while baking; the cheese is pungent!
  4. Sprinkle cheese with thyme and pepper to serve.

    5. Try serving with roasted winter root vegetables, crusty bread, or cornichons!
