Lithuania

Šaltibarščiai

A cold beef soup from Lithuania for the summer that can be served with potatoes or other dishes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 4
  • Buttermilk: 1 quart
  • Beets, peeled and shredded: 1 Ib.
  • Large English Cucumber, peeled, quartered, and sliced: 1
  • Chives, minced: 1/4 cup
  • Bunch Fresh Dill, minced: 1

Steps

  1. Place the eggs into a saucepan in a single layer and cover the eggs with water by 1 inch. Cover the saucepan and bring the water to a boil.
  2. Remove from the heat and let the eggs stand in the hot water for 15 minutes; drain. Cool the eggs under cold running water in the sink. Peel and chop the eggs.
  3. Pour the buttermilk into a large bowl; add the eggs, beets, cucumber, chives, and dill. Stir gently to combine. Chill in refrigerator for 1 full day before serving.

Source: Lithuanian Saltibarsciai (Cold Beet Soup)
